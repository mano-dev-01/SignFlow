Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is SignFlow?

SignFlow achieves 85-90% accuracy on recognized signs. Accuracy improves with clear lighting and stable hand visibility.

Does it work with all video platforms?

SignFlow works with any screen-based video feed including Zoom, Teams, YouTube, and recorded videos.

What about other sign languages?

The current version supports ASL. We're working on BSL, LSF, and other sign languages.

Is there a cost?

SignFlow is free and open-source. We accept donations to support development.
